Introduction

Super Smash Brothers is a series of platform-based fighting games created by Nintendo. They are popular with both children and adults alike. In the games, you can play as a variety of characters from different Nintendo games throughout history.

The most recent game in the series for the Wii-U is known as "Smash 4" by fans, as it is the fourth game in the series, after Super Smash Bros 64, Super Smash Bros Melee, and Super Smash Bros Brawl.
